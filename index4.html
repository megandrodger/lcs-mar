If your tenant doesn’t have documents

If any of the following conditions apply, you must use the Home Office right to rent check (formally known as ‘landlord’s checking service’) to check whether the tenant is allowed to rent without the right documents.

The tenant:

  • has an outstanding application with the Home Office or the Home Office has their documents
  • has lost their documents
  • says the Home Office gave them ‘permission to rent’

    • You’ll get an reply from us within 2 working days.

    Don’t rent a property to someone in England if they don’t have the right documents and the Home Office right to rent check tells you they aren’t allowed to rent.

    Call the landlords helpline if you have questions about a right to rent check.
