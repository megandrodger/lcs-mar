Can you make a report to the Home Office?

Tick the boxes to check you can use this service:

  • The rental property is in England

  • You carried out a right to rent check at the start of the tenancy

  • Your tenant failed a right to rent follow-up check

  • The tenancy started after 1 February 2016 (or 1 December 2014 in the West Midlands)

  • Your prospective tenant / tenant has given you permission to share their details with the Home Office
