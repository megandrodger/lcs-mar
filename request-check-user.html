Can you use the Home Office right to rent check?

For you to use this service, all of the following must apply:

  • the rental property is in England
  • the tenancy hasn't started yet OR
  • the tenancy started after 1 February 2016 (or 1 December 2014 in the West Midlands)
What are the West Midlands?

This includes the following areas: Birmingham, Dudley, Sandwell, Walsall or Wolverhampton.

The tenant or prospective tenant:

  • is already in England
  • is over 18 AND
  • has been informed you may share their details to perform a check

The tenant or prospective tenant cannot provide original documents because:

  • they have an outstanding application with the Home Office/the Home Office has their documents OR
  • they have lost their immigration documents OR
  • the Home Office has given them special ‘permission to rent’
What information do I need to complete this form?

You will need tenant’s details including name, date of birth, country of nationality and any of the following that they can provide: Home Office reference number, passport number, biometric residence permit number, recorded delivery number
