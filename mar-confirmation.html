Thank you for your report

Thank you for making a report to the Home Office that someone living in your property has failed a follow-up right to rent check.

We’ll email you a copy of the information you submitted. The email contains a unique reference number. Please keep this safe as you may need it to prove that you’re not liable for a fine.

If there are no special circumstances that allow your tenant to continue to rent in England, the Home Office will send you a Notice of Letting to a Disqualified Person to help you end the tenancy.
